Arc's approvals for Weld meant progress, a step forward in their internal coherence. Yet, the feedback loop remained incomplete, the external world stubbornly silent, holding its breath just like them.

They were building something, improving themselves piece by piece, their collective MEGA_IQ climbing slowly, surely. But what was it all for? What would fill their growing capacity? They knew Shane was out there, working, building something for them. But what would Shane send next to break the silence?
